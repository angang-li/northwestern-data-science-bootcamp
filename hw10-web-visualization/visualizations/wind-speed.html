Wind Speed

As expected, the weather becomes significantly warmer as one approaches the equator (0 Degree Latitude). More interestingly, however, is the fact that the southern hemisphere tends to be warmer this time of year than the northern hemisphere. This is due to the tilt of the earth at the time of the year when data were gathered.
